Our principles

We teach children knowledge that has practical value today. An individual approach to each child, taking into account their characteristics and needs, makes us unique among other educational institutions.


Our team includes over 80 qualified teachers, including native English speakers. They use the best educational practices from Western Europe and the USA to ensure that our students receive relevant and necessary knowledge.


With the beginning of the full-scale invasion of the Russian Federation, our school actively participated in the humanitarian project “School without Walls”, initiated by the NGO “Smart Education”. We organized online learning for 150 Ukrainian schoolchildren, including children who lost their homes and families. This initiative inspired us to expand volunteer activities and create scholarship programs for gifted and motivated students.

Our classes

Small classes of 5-8 students allow us to work with each student in a personalized manner and provide the necessary attention.
